
# 🍔 Swiggy Late Delivery Reduction — Product Case Study

> **Role:** Product Manager | **Timeline:** 2 weeks | **Status:** PRD Complete

## 📊 The Problem
Swiggy delivers **88% of orders on time**. The remaining 12% (≈1.2M orders/month) cost **₹48Cr annually** in refunds and cause **15% lower repeat rates** after 2+ late deliveries.

## 🎯 My Impact (Projected)
- **On-time delivery:** 88% → 94% (+6%)
- **Customer wait time:** 42 min → 35 min (-7 min)
- **Support tickets:** ↓40%
- **Annual impact:** ₹48Cr saved

## 🛠️ Solutions Delivered

| Feature | Impact |
|---------|--------|
| Proactive delay comms + ₹25 coupon | Support tickets ↓40% |
| Smart rider auto-reassign | Pickup wait ↓3 min |
| Restaurant prep scoring | Prep delays 42% → 25% |
