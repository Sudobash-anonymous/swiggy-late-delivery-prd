# User Personas: Swiggy Late Delivery

## Persona 1: Rahul — The Office Team Lead

**Demographics**
- Age: 28
- Occupation: Software Engineer at Google
- Location: Bangalore (Koramangala)
- Order frequency: 3x/week (lunch for team)

**Goals**
- Get food exactly when meeting ends
- Look good in front of team (no awkward waiting)

**Pain Points**
- "I order at 12:45 PM for 1:15 PM delivery. It comes at 1:30 PM and everyone stares at me."
- "I can't expense the ₹25 coupon — it's not worth the embarrassment."

**Behaviors**
- Tracks order obsessively (refreshes 10+ times)
- Switches to Zomato after 2 late deliveries

**Needs from this project**
- Honest ETA upfront
- Proactive notification BEFORE meeting ends

**Quote**
> *"Just tell me 1:30 PM if it's going to be 1:30 PM. Don't say 1:15 PM and make me lie to my team."*

## Persona 2: Priya — The Working Mom

**Demographics**
- Age: 35
- Occupation: Marketing Manager
- Location: Mumbai (Andheri)
- Order frequency: 5x/week (dinner for kids)

**Goals**
- Feed kids before 8 PM (bedtime routine)
- No cooking after 12-hour workday

**Pain Points**
- "Kids get cranky when food is late. One late order ruins the whole evening."
- "I've stopped ordering from restaurants that are consistently late."

**Behaviors**
- Orders at 6:30 PM for 7:30 PM delivery (buffer built in)
- Calls support aggressively when late

**Needs from this project**
- Automatic compensation (no call to support)
- Ability to cancel without fee if delay >15 min

**Quote**
> *"I don't have 20 minutes to chat with support. Just give me my ₹50 coupon and let me feed my kids."*

**Needs from this project**

- No penalty if I cancel when order is late

**Quote**
> *"I'll literally sleep on the street if your ETA is wrong. Please fix this."*

## Persona 4: Suresh — The Rider

**Demographics**
- Age: 32
- Occupation: Full-time Swiggy rider
- Location: Delhi (Rohini)
- Weekly earnings target: ₹12,000

**Goals**
- Maximize ₹/hour (currently ₹180)
- Avoid unfair penalties

**Pain Points**
- "Restaurant makes me wait 15 minutes, then Swiggy penalizes ME for late delivery."
- "I can't afford to reject orders, but sometimes I get stuck in impossible traffic."

**Behaviors**
- Accepts 95% of orders
- Works 10 AM - 10 PM, 6 days/week
- Has 2 phones (one for Swiggy, one for Zomato)

**Needs from this project**
- Clear "not my fault" option
- Extra pay for taking over delayed orders

**Quote**
> *"Give me ₹15 extra for every order where the restaurant was late. I'll take every single one."*

## Persona 5: Meera — Restaurant Owner

**Demographics**
- Age: 42
- Business: Burger King franchise owner
- Location: Pune (Koregaon Park)
- Monthly orders: 3,000 (Swiggy)

**Goals**
- Maximize profit per order
- Maintain 4.0+ rating

**Pain Points**
- "Peak hour (8-9 PM) — every order takes 8-10 min extra. Swiggy's ETA doesn't account for this."
- "I get penalized for things outside my control (rider no-show, traffic)."

**Behaviors**
- Manually marks orders "ready" even when not (to avoid penalty)
- Calls riders directly to speed up pickup

**Needs from this project**
- Fair prep time scoring (adjust for peak hours)
- Visibility into what's hurting my score

**Quote**
*> "I'm not lying about prep time to be evil. I'm doing it because Swiggy's system punishes honesty."*

## Persona 6: Neha — Support Agent

**Demographics**
- Age: 24
- Role: Senior support agent
- Location: Hyderabad (remote)
- Tickets handled: 80/day

**Goals**
- Resolve tickets in <3 minutes
- Maintain 90% CSAT

**Pain Points**
- "50% of my tickets are 'Where's my order?' — I have no visibility into root cause."
- "Customers yell at ME when the restaurant or rider caused the delay."

**Behaviors**
- Has 6 canned responses for delivery delays
- Manually issues ₹25-50 coupons (no automation)

**Needs from this project**
- Dashboard showing root cause (restaurant/rider/traffic)
- Auto-coupons (so customer doesn't call)

**Quote**
> *"Just tell me who messed up. I'll handle the rest."*
