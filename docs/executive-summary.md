# Executive Summary (1-Page)

## Problem
Swiggy's on-time delivery is 88% — 1.2M late orders/month costing ₹48Cr annually.

## Solution (3 features)

| # | Feature | Impact |
|---|---------|--------|
| 1 | Proactive customer comms + ₹25 coupon | Support tickets ↓40% |
| 2 | Auto-reassign stalled riders | Pickup wait ↓3 min |
| 3 | Restaurant prep scoring (0-100) | Prep delays 42% → 25% |

## Timeline
- **Week 1-2:** Build P0 features
- **Week 3-4:** Bangalore rollout (2% → 100%)
- **Week 5:** Top 5 cities
- **Week 6:** Nationwide

## Success Metrics
- **North Star:** 94% on-time delivery (+6%)
- **Secondary:** 35 min avg wait (-7 min)
- **Guardrails:** Rider churn <10%

## ROI
- **Investment:** ~₹8Cr (engineering, product, ops)
- **Annual savings:** ₹29Cr (refunds + support)
- **ROI:** 3.6x in Year 1

## Go/No-Go Criteria
✅ On-time rate >90% in Bangalore pilot  
✅ Support tickets ↓30%  
✅ Rider churn <10%  
✅ Restaurant rejection rate <5%
